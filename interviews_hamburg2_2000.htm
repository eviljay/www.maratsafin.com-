20th 0f May 2000 - Interview with Marat Safin on Sunday
during Tennis Master Series 2000 in Hamburg


Question: Do you think the first set was the strongest you had to play since the beginning of the tournament?

Safin: It was a little bit different, because I started very badly. I started like 3:0 down. I was completely out of the court, I missed easy balls from the baseline. Afterwards I just started to think, I just concentrated and I was trying to calm down and to realize the situation. After that I had 0:30 on his serve, twice I think, I had a good chance, but I was too nervous at that moment to play the way I should have played these two balls. I had two easy balls and I missed them.

I was a little bit upset. All the time I had 0:30 on his serve, and two easy balls, so after I lost the game I got a little bit disappointed. But after the tie-break I played my game. I was unlucky and he was three points up, but I tried to put the balls in. Why not, I had nothing to lose on the set. I just pushed him twice and I took the right decisions and sometimes I played unbelievable shots. I got him under pressure and he missed a few balls. That's it, then he was gone. After the tie-break I got confident in the second set and I just had to return his serve, that's it. After that I was with a lot of confidence.

Q: Was that an example of a match that you would have lost during the first four months of the year, because you would have got down in the first set?

Safin: I'm not too sure if I would have lost the first set. He's also a player, if I lose the first set it would have not been a tragedy. I had to play and I'm ready to play. I have my game and I'm not afraid to play a three set match. I have the serve, I have almost everything, but I'm not scared. I'm not scared to fight against Marcelo. I know he is a great player, he was number one in the world. But still, I also know how to play tennis sometimes, you know. I'm not scared, I'm trying to play my game all the time. That's important, because if my game doesn't work, what can I do? What can you do? Nothing. But you have to be always under control. You have to know what you have to do and try to play your game all the time.

Q: You're playing Guga tomorrow. Can you something about that?

Safin: I know I play against him. It will be a difficult match, because I know that he wants to win this match, because I beat him three times. We went to the fifth set twice and once we went to the third set into the tie-break. I hope it will not be tough for me, but I think it will be tough. I will just try to build my game every time, just be calm and I have to be confident with what I'm doing on the court. Everything under control, you know. I will try to do the same as this week, because my game was all the time under control. If I lose, I won't be upset, because I'm doing a great job. I'm playing at a great level. But I hope I will do my best tomorrow, I will give everything to beat him, because it is a final of a Super 9, well of the Masters Series, sorry. It's not a match of the first round, it's a final, so we'll see tomorrow who is the man.

Q: You beat him three times. What was your opinion why you beat him?

Safin: I still don't know, because it's always a tough match, just two or three points difference. But sometimes I was lucky and sometimes I made the right decisions at the important moments. Okay, I know how to play against him, but they were always tough matches. I was just lucky, I think.

Q: This is your third final in four week. Are you amazed by that?

Safin: No, why not? I work for it. It's great, it's coming up now. I've worked for six months and I was looking for my game and finally I found it. I hope I won't lose it again for the next six months. I hope it won't happen again. We'll see. It's great that I won two tournaments and that I'm in the final here. But I need something more, you always need more, like everybody, it's never enough.

Q: You're concentrating on your game, but do you also take the time to do other things as well here in Hamburg?

Safin: Yes, good food, nice city and also good persons around me. It's a good atmosphere. I'm happy to be here.

Q: You had tremendous success on clay court. Do you consider yourself to be one of the favourites for Roland Garros?

A: It's still one week to go until Roland Garros, and also Kuerten made it to two finals already, so we'll see, maybe he will win tomorrow. I don't think so, but there are more guys, like Norman and a few guys more. I think it would be nice if I was one of them, one of the favourites for Roland Garros. But I prefer to be nobody, so nobody expects a lot from me. Nobody is putting me under pressure, you know, that you have to win and you have be one of the favourites. Well, the time will come to see who is the favourite. But it's a tougher tournament. It's a big event and everybody wants to win there, because you get a lot of points. So everybody is going to fight. First of all you have to pass the first round there, and afterwards we will see.

Q: Do you feel that you've improved your game since Barcelona?

Safin: No, I think I'm on the same level. Just some things changed, but I finally understand how I have to play. So I know what I have to do and I know my mistakes now. I can realize the situation which is very important. Of course I have to improve, but not now. But when I have time to work like in two or three weeks. Then we can talk after that if I have improved something. Right now I do the things in the right way, that's it.

Q: You don't seem to be too happy about your win. Come on, tomorrow could be the best day of your life.

Safin: What do you want me to do, you want me to jump around here or what? The final is nice, but you don't have to be too happy, because tomorrow it's a final. After the final we will see how happy I will be. If I lose I will be also happy, because I made the way up. And if I win the tournament I will be happy because I'll be top ten, I hope so, and a few things more. But now I prefer to think about the match of tomorrow. And afterwards, we'll see, we're going to have a party for sure, definitely. If you want to hear this, okay. But for the moment not, because I'm still in the tournament and I don't want to lose this opportunity to win this tournament. I have some chances, I think. I'll wait until tomorrow, and then I'll do all these things.
