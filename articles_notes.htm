Safin without socks

Borrowing from Kiefer and Tarango

Marat Safin had to resort to desperate measures during his third round match against Sebastien Grosjean. With the rain wetting all his clothes, the Russian powerhouse was forced to borrow a fresh pair of socks from Nicolas Kiefer of Germany during the first rain delay and borrowed a pair from Jeff Tarango during the second. "I hope I will never be in the same situation because it was terrible," he said.

08/09/2000 - US Open New-York, USA
