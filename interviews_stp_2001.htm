Here are some interviews of Marat Safin from St.Petersburg 2001 tournament in Russia.
Interviews translated by Maxim (maxasia).


10.28.2001 St.Petersburg, Russia
Marat Safin - Rainer Schuettler 3-6, 6-3, 6-3.
interview after the match against Schuettler


- Your comments about final?
- It was tough. I’ve played 6 sets yesterday and all weren’t easy. Spent about 12 hours on the court. Some more, my knee hurts after I hit myself with the racket during practice. So I was feeling terrible.

- Major of SPB made a mistake during his speech and called you Rinat instead of Marat. Did you want to react some way?
- Nothing serious. Anybody can make a mistake. I’ve used to it.

- YK after winning 4 times Kreml. Cup has mentioned he may consider buying out the tournament. How many times you have to win here to come out with the same idea?
- Are you trying to ask me how much money I have to make for that? I think even if you are investing a huge amount of money into business venture it doesn’t mean you are going to have nice feeling about it. It all depends on love from spectators, not on the finances.

- Last year you had A.Volkov on the stand to support you here. This year he isn’t among spectators. Did you feel any support from him?
- I have a very close relationship with Alexander. I have no secrets from him. It’s OK he isn’t here today. I still consider him as a best friend.

- You have made so many mistakes in the 1st set. Is it related to your tough matches during past few days?
- As I said earlier, I’ve spent too much time on the court. Slept 8 hours. Felt pain in my knee, couldn’t move well. But I’ve managed to pull myself together and win a couple of opponent’s service games. That’s why I won.

- Any comments on line judges tonight?
- It’s true, there was a couple of disputable calls. It’s pity, when you are playing and home and your serve is in (clearly for everybody) but it is called out. Every point is very important and it’s not nice such a bad mistakes can happen.

- Last year you were irritated by spectator’s ringing mobile phones. Any comments on spectators “behavior” this year?
- It is looks like people made more money and now they have phones with vibration mode. It is nice to see that Russians are doing well and prosper. (Laugh in audience).

- Did you manage to see anything else in SPB besides courts and hotel?
- Unfortunately, no.

- Any prediction on your performance in Paris and chances to go to Sydney?
- I don’t have a good chance for Sydney. But all can change if I win in Paris. But it will depend not only on my physical condition but how others going to play there. For instance, Tim Henman. It’s OK if Paris going to be my last tournament of the year. I’m very glad I’ve won two titles this season. But of course, I will try to perform better in future.

- Did you ever get so much prizes and presents after wining a tournament? What are you going to do with it?
- I have no idea, but I think it’s going to be a nice trouble.



10.27.2001 St.Petersburg, Russia
Marat Safin - Yevgeny Kafelnikov 6-7(4), 6-2, 6-4.
interview after the match against Kefelnikov


- Tonight both of you were disagree with line calls very often. What’s happened?
- Not only players but also judges were nervous as well. They have made mistakes a couple of times. It is normal. If you think you are right you should tell this to empire. He needs to keep concentration too. It vas a very close match and 2-3 points could decide a winner. So price for mistakes was very high.

- How YK has surprised you tonight?
- He concentrates well and has started great. He is in great form now and it is difficult to play against him. He is playing too fast and it is causing problems for his opponents.

- It was a very close match, in second and third sets score was 40:40 very often on your serve. How did you win? Is it concentration, determination or luck?
- I think I didn’t serve well. It was difficult to play without good serve against such a strong opponent as YK (some more he has a great return). I was lucky sometimes.

- You had a few foot faults, which is seldom happened to you. Any comments?
- For the past 3 month I had no foot fault at all. Normally if foot fault in mm or cm it won’t help you much and judges usually don’t call it. So I was surprised with so many such calls tonight. I think two or three times I even didn’t cross the line. Some more I’ve been serving badly, everybody saw it.

- Can you say that after two win in a row against YK you will have psychological advantage against him in future?
- YK is very strong. I always know he is going to give 100% on the court. He is concentrating great and serving good. His serve not his strongest weapon but today everything was in place for him. Everything was decided by one point. He could win tonight. Very tough match to get any psychological advantage.

- Both of you wanted to play great tennis, winning was second priority. So, no goal to win by any cost?
- It was our first match in Russia. Before we played couple of years ago in Rome and this year in Tashkent. We wanted to give a good performance for spectators. We both played well and deserve applauses. If anyone win too easily it wouldn’t be so interesting. Tonight we both have given our best.

- Did you book candlelight dinner wit YK? After such performance you deserve it.
- Thanks. I think everybody happy. Good match. I’m proud of YK and myself. We have played for spectators only.


10.25.2001 St.Petersburg, Russia
Marat Safin - Alberto Martin 6-7, 6-4, 7-6.
interview after the match against Martin


- Martin has a lot of patience and stays back a lot. It looks like it this type of game you have made more mistake than he. What’s the reason?
- It’s difficult to play with type of balls, which have been chosen for SPBO. You can call its “dead”. Not suitable for slice shots at all. But I’ve found match very useful. I’ve been trying to gain confidence and even in wasn’t easy, I think I’ve found my game, especially before Paris.

- A lot of questions if you can make it to Sydney. Is it so important for you?
- Not so much. To go there I need to win or at least to be in the SF here and win in Paris. With kind of tennis I play now it is difficult to get to Sydney. But since I’m gaining confidence and if play well in Paris – who knows? There is a chance but I’m not too concern about it. For me important thing is to be in the top 5 and not lower. The rest is not for me. So my priority is to gain necessary confidence and try to be number 1 at the end of 2002.

- What about spectators? Looks like some of them were against you tonight.
- I didn’t notice anything like this. Opposite, I’m very happy with crowd’s support. They have seen my mistake, knew I’m not playing my best, but still supported me. I’m grateful they understand tennis so well.

- Your participation in Kreml. Cup – did it gain more supporters for you?
- I don’t want to talk about it anymore, but OK, I will answer last time. It’s impossible always to win and display great tennis. I’m not robot – just human being. When you are not on top of your game, you expect support not scolding. I had match point against Hrbaty, I’ve been fighting to the end, played for spectators. And when they blame you – it is not nice, ridiculous, and stupid.

- Seems it is spectators fault, for instance, they didn’t turn up for latest “Spartak” game in Champions League. So, no matter what, they have to give their full support?
- No, they have to throw chairs… “Spartak” are not playing well at this moment but guys were trying. They were among top 8 in Europe last season. I’ve been living in Spain for a few years and when “Real” Madrid has been hanging around 10th place, stadium always was 100% full.

- Your next opponent is GI. What is your opinion?
- I’ve played against him once at Wimbledon, lost in 4 tight sets. It is difficult to play against him on the fast surface – he has a great serve. I will try my best; I think 2-3 points will decide it. Hope I will be able to return well and a lot will depend on luck.

- You supposed to pay to Mats for every broken racket. Today you have been doing it often.
- I didn’t keep my promise, I break rackets often, and it is ridicules if I pay every time. It is twice more upsetting since I have to spend money to buy a new one.
