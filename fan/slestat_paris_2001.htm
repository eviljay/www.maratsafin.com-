Paris 2001 reports by SLestat





I wanted to get back on Marat's match that I attended yesterday with Denise and Ilaria among other fans.
What was that ???
First of all it happened on court n°1.
Why???
Marat is the one who won the title last year he deserved to play on center court.
But…
But Guga, Grosjean, and Arazi played the same day…

Kuerten with his 3 titles in RG is considered as a major player by the organizers who think that he has many fans in France. But I'm sure Marat is more popular among the French public. I know a lot of fans of his and above all I remember how the crowd was devoted to him last year during his final against Philippoussis.

Of course Grosjean is French and he will certainly go to Sydney so perhaps he's got an excuse. And eventually if Arazi was broadcast on center court it is certainly because he speaks French and has a French coach (Thierry Champion). So I guess they preferred nationalistic considerations to fairness as far as the results are concerned….silly….

Let's go back to court n°1. The good point is that as there are no booked seats there, so we succeeded in being in the first row. But what a cold atmosphere there !! The court is installed on a skating rink that has been transformed just for the tournament. So it's cold and dark, but hopefully the fans were here to make the fever rise. (and perhaps too much sometimes: once I thought that the girls behind me were going to hit the supporters of Vinciguerra.)

I was really excited about seeing Marat so close, and also Mats who seemed to me very worried. But the match was a torture for all Marat's fan. And also for the umpires… Before the match starts I heard two of them chatting and laughing: "Oh no !! not Safin, He's going to shoot us as usual (addressing to the public): does anyone want to take my seat?". It's true that Marat's serves are really impressive ! I was just in the prolongation of the line that separates the court in two parts, and three times in the match I felt as if Marat's balls where going to decapitate me as there is no protection such as a net to protect the public. _by the way during the former match my neighbour was hit in the middle of the chest and it took him a few seconds to recover. It's dangerous to attend the TMS in Paris… ;-)

The match seemed really short to me. And surprisingly quite boring too…. I had come there to see Marat playing, not to see Marat waiting for Vinciguerra's mistakes !!. From the third games, when he got broken and smashed his racket we had understood that he was no longer involved in the match: he was just undergoing Vinciguerra's law; and it was pathetic. It went so fast that I didn't even take many pictures. A dozen not more. But was it worth shooting a disappointed Marat ? I don't think so. Anyway I'll scan them and send them to you as soon as I'll have them.

I am also really disappointed by this tournament, and I almost don't want to attend the semifinals tomorrow, but I'd like to see Kafelnikov because I've never seen him live, moreover he's the last major player with Grosjean who remain in the draw.

So to finish with, I have one wish: May Roland Garros come really quickly with its fine weather, its charming village, its supporting public, and above all a new Marat, fit, fresh, convincing and triumphant !!

Slestat for all Marat's fans.
