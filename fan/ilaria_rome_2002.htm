Rome 2002 report by Ilaria





MY REPORT FROM ROME 2002

FIRST DAY 6 OF MAY

THE FIRST DAY AT TENNIS MASTER SERIES IN ROME WAS GREAT...MARAT PLAYED IN CENTRE COURT AT 1.00 PM....AT 11 AM. HE STARTED THE TRAINING WITH MARC ROSSET AND AMIT.
I WAS THE ONLY PERSON TO WATCH HIM....GREAT!!!AT THE END OF HIS TRAINING I SPOKE WITH MARAT,I GAVE HIM A PRESENT AND I ASKED HOW HE WAS,MARAT SAD ME THAT HE FELT GOOD AND THAT HE RECOGNIZED ME.....I WAS SO HAPPY ABOUT IT!
HE SAD ME MANY TIMES THANKS FOR THE PRESENT AND THEN HE WENT AWAY...
AT 12.30 PM. I WAS IN CENTRE COURT,FIRST ROW NEAR THE PLAYERS SEATS.MARAT PLAYED SO WELL,GREAT SHOTS....HE WAS TERRIFIC WITH HIS BACKHAND!!!

SECOND DAY 7 OF MAY

MARAT TODAY PLAYED JUST IN DOUBLE WITH CANAS...WITH MY SURPRISE AT 2.00 PM. THERE WAS A SPECIAL MEETING WITH MARAT FOR ALL HIS FANS TO SIGN AUTOGRAPHS......THERE WERE A LOT OF KIDS AND MARAT WAS VERY HAPPY ABOUT IT,HE STAYED WITH US 30 MINUTES TO SPEAK AND TAKE PHOTOS.
I WATCHED HIS TRAINING,AND WHEN HE SAW ME SAD:HELLO! WITH HIS WONDERFUL SMILE...
AT 4 PM. STARTED THE MATCH,BUT MARAT AND CANAS LOST EASILY 6-0 6-3...BUT MARAT WASN'T VERY SAD,HE NEEDED TO CONCENTRATE ON MALISSE...
MARC ROSSET WAS TO WATCH THE MATCH,AMIT TOO,BUT HE SLEPT FOR ALL THE MATCH...IT WAS SO FUNNY!
THIRD DAY 8 OF MAY
TODAY MARAT PLAYS IN COURT NUMBER 6,BETTER FOR ME...HE IS SO CLOSE AGAIN!
IT WAS RAINING,SO THE MATCHES STARTED AT 4 PM. AND ALSO THE PROGRAM HAS CHANGED...MARAT MALISSE WAS THE THIRD MATCH IN COURT NUMBER 5......NEVER MIND GOOD COURT TO SEE THE PLAYERS VERY WELL!
THE MATCH STARTED VERY LATE,AT 9.30 PM.,I WAS NEAR AMIT,ROSSET AND 4 RUSSIAN MARAT'S FRIENDS....VERY NICE.
IT WAS A STRANGE MATCH,MARAT CAN'T PLAY...HE SPOKE WITH HIMSELF...SAING THAT HE CAN'T PLAY...HE DIDN'T KNOW WHY...ANYWAY HE MADE A BEAUTIFUL SHOW...SMASH RACKETS,HE TRIED TO BREAK UP HIS TROUSERS LOOKING TAWORDS ME SMILING....
IT WAS INCREDIBLE BUT I QUARRELED WITH MALISSE DURING THE MATCH....I MEAN,I SHOUTED:GO MARAT GO MARAT...AND MALISSE MADE FUN OF ME REPETING:GO MARAT GO MARAT WITH A STUPID VOICE.....MARAT WAS IRRITATED ABOUT MALISSE'S BEHAVIOUR.......AND ME TOO!
DURING ALL THE SECOND SET ROSSET GAVE A LOT OF ADVICES TO MARAT,BUT MARAT SAD:OH MARC FOR ME IS ENOUGH THIS EVENING...IT'S OK...
AT THE END OF THE MATCH MARAT GAVE ME HIS TOWEL AND SAD ME BYE....AND I CAN SAY THAT HE WASNT VERY HAPPY ABOUT THAT LOST....
THE ROME ADVENTURE IS OVER,I'M SO SAD BECAUSE MARAT LOST,BUT I'M HAPPY TO HAVE HAD THE CHANCE TO SEE HIM AGAIN AND TO HAVE HAD A LITTLE CONTACT WITH HIM......

ILARIA
