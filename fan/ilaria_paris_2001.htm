Paris 2001 report by Ilaria




MY REPORT ABOUT PARIS

Hi everybody
I'm come back from Paris yesterday night,so now I'm here to write how I feel after that wonderful week with Denise and Chris.

As you know from Denise's reports we met Marat on tuesday,wednesday.thursday and friday,and everytime I took a photo with him…and some others photos that I will send to Sergei as soon as possible.

The firs day that we met him out of the hotel I asked if he remebered me and the present that I gave to him at Wimbledon(the gold pendant that he has on his chain)and Marat sad:oh yes,I really liked it!While we took the photo together I put my hand over his ass and I sad:oh I 'm really sorry!but Marat sad me:oh no,no problem!!!I can't believe it what a wonderful ass!!!

Every day I took a photo with him and a lot of photo during the matches…we were always in the first row…and at the end of the first match,Marat turned to us and sad Bye .During the second match in court number 1…he looked at us like to have some more support…he was so close!!!

The last day that we saw him was fantastic…he came out from the hotel,he checked his bags inside the car and after that I asked:Marat can I have my last photo with you?and he sad:oh sure,come on!and he hugged me…after I sad him bye bye and I asked a kiss and Marat very kindli sad:yes!

He hugged me and kissed me two times…it was a wonderful sensation,I have always his smell on my mind…I sad then:see you in Monte carlo,and Marat sad:ok,it is a pleasure.

I will never forget that week,Marat was so kind with me…what happened is unbelievable,and I hope to meet him again and again…
