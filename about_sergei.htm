About this web site and me


Hi there!
Thanks for visiting my web site and spending some time to read it.
My name is Sergei. I was born in Moscow, Russia, but have been living in Amsterdam, the Netherlands for more than 10 years now.
It's obvious that I'm a big fan of Marat Safin. I have been keeping up with his career for a while. His talent really impresses me, and of course he is from Russia like I am.
After having found some sites on the Net about him, I was disappointed, and wanted to know more. So I decided to make my own independent unofficial Marat Safin web site.
The idea of this web site is to compile and catalogue information about Marat Safin which I find on the Internet and in tennis magazines. I make this web site for fans of Marat Safin just like I am.
This web site is my hobby and I make it in my free time.
It's a serious web site..... and hopefully not too boring. I'll try do my best to keep things lively and updated. And I hope that Marat will keep up his part of the bargain and give me something to write about.

Sergei
